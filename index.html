About Child Welfare News

Child Welfare News is a free content aggregator bringing together news, research, toolkits and policy updates from across the child welfare ecosystem — so practitioners, researchers, and policymakers don't have to go looking across a dozen different sites to stay current.

Articles are pulled from sources including ACF, The Imprint, the Annie E. Casey Foundation, First Focus, Child Welfare Monitor, and more, alongside broader news search.

This project was born out of a need for accessible, uncensored information. Child Welfare News exists to ensure that access to information, including equity, justice, and systemic change, remains open, transparent, and available to everyone, no matter the political climate.
