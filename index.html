About Child Welfare News

Child Welfare News is a free content aggregator bringing together news, research, and policy updates from across the child welfare ecosystem — so practitioners, researchers, and policymakers don't have to go looking across a dozen different sites to stay current.

Articles are pulled from trusted sources including The Imprint, the Annie E. Casey Foundation, Child Welfare Monitor, NCCPR, and Children's Rights, alongside broader news search.
